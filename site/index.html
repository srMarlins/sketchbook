Already have it? Auto-update picks up new versions on next launch — nothing to do.

Every save, kept.

Hits Ctrl-S, gets a snapshot. Rewind to any moment, restore samples that wandered off, see exactly what changed.

Sync across devices.

One source of truth, content-addressed. Open the same project on your studio rig and your laptop without "which one was newer?"

Repair the chaos.

Surfaces broken Mac paths, missing samples, duplicates. Proposes fixes you approve in batches. The library stays browsable.

Who is this for?

Producers with hundreds (or, in my case, ~1,628) of half-finished Ableton sketches scattered across drives. Personal use; private libraries. Not a DAW. Not a chat tool. A tactile catalog that makes the chaos feel inviting again.
